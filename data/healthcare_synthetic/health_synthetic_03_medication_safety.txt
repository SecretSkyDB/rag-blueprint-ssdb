SYNTHETIC MEDICATION SAFETY SNIPPET — DEMONSTRATION DATA ONLY. NOT MEDICAL ADVICE.

General concepts sometimes covered in public health handouts:
- Use one primary pharmacy so interactions can be screened in one system.
- Keep a written list in your wallet, including over-the-counter drugs and supplements.
- Do not share prescription medications; doses are individualized.
- Some medications require taking with food; others on an empty stomach—follow the label and prescriber instructions.
- When starting something new, ask: what is it for, how long, what side effects to watch for, and what to do if a dose is missed.
- Return unused drugs through approved take-back programs when available.
